How we work

  • We're startup people, which means we have extensive hands-on experience of developing new software products with limited resources, in a context of high uncertainty.
  • We learned a bit from our successes, a lot from our mistakes, and we're still learning every day.
  • We know the buzzwords (Lean Startup / Customer Development, Agile / SCRUM / XP...) but we're pragmatists, which means we like to use the right tools for the job.
  • You can hire us to help you on specific assignments, but we like to develop long-term relationships with our customers, acting as your trusted technical advisor.
  • If you're a startup, you can think of us as your part-time chief technical officer (CTO).
